Evaluating Your Social Media Needs

Every client has a different set of business goals and objectives. To ensure our social media clients get the maximum value out of their time with us and that we deliver for them, we go through an evaluation process to decide which social media service is most suitable for their present needs along with agreed targets.






Goals & Objectives

We initially consult with our client
to decide what goals and objectives
they would like social media to help
deliver for their business or brand.






Current Activity

We review all current social media
activity including content, consistency
of messaging, calls to action, frequency
of posting and platforms used.






Services Required

We then propose the most beneficial
social media service such as platform
training sessions, campaign managements
or strategy sessions.






Execute & Review

We then deliver the agreed social media
services based on specific goals and
objectives which can then be evaluated after a
specified time period.
